Questions Welcome

Ask a Pastor

You do not need a polished question. If something about faith, Scripture, suffering, salvation, baptism, or church life is confusing, you can ask honestly.

1

No performance required.

Sometimes the hardest part of asking a question is feeling like you should already know the answer. You are free to come plainly. We would rather you ask than quietly carry confusion alone.

2

Ask about what actually matters.

Questions about Jesus, the Bible, salvation, doubt, grief, baptism, membership, prayer, and next steps are all welcome. If your question is messy, that is okay too.

3

What happens next?

Your message goes to church leadership. Someone will follow up personally when a response is needed. For urgent pastoral care, please call the church office directly.
