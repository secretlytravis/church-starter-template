We Are Praying For You

Prayer Requests

We believe God hears and answers prayer. Whatever you are carrying right now — we would be honored to carry it with you before the Lord.

Philippians 4:6–7

"Do not be anxious about anything, but in everything by prayer and supplication with thanksgiving let your requests be made known to God. And the peace of God, which surpasses all understanding, will guard your hearts and your minds in Christ Jesus."

How Can We Pray for You?

Prayer is not a formality here — it is something we take seriously. When you submit a request, it goes to our pastoral team and is brought before the Lord in prayer. We do not take that lightly.

You are welcome to share as much or as little as you are comfortable with. If your situation is sensitive, you can submit anonymously or mark your request as confidential — it will be prayed over privately by our pastor and not shared with the wider congregation.

If your need is urgent or you would like someone to reach out to you personally, please let us know in your request and we will be in touch.

A note on confidentiality Requests marked confidential are seen only by our pastoral team and are never shared publicly or from the pulpit without your permission.

Thank You

Your prayer request has been received. We will be praying for you.
