For Your Growth

Resources

Recommended books and trusted online resources for every stage of the Christian life — from brand new believer to seasoned saint.

2 Peter 3:18

"But grow in the grace and knowledge of our Lord and Savior Jesus Christ."

Romans 12:2

"Do not be conformed to this world, but be transformed by the renewal of your mind, that by testing you may discern what is the will of God, what is good and acceptable and perfect."

The Christian life does not stand still. Peter's command to grow is not a suggestion — it is a call to ongoing, intentional pursuit of deeper knowledge of Christ and His Word. A faith that stops growing is a faith that begins to drift.

Paul's vision in Romans 12 is of a believer whose mind is being actively renewed — shaped by Scripture rather than by the surrounding culture. That kind of transformation does not happen by accident. It requires reading, thinking, sitting under faithful preaching, and returning again and again to the Word. The resources on this page are tools for that work — not substitutes for Scripture, but faithful guides that point you back to it.
